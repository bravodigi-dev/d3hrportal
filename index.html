HR KNOWLEDGE CENTRE

Policies and common questions

How do I apply for leave?

Log in to the Faith HR System, select the leave type, enter the dates and submit the request for manager approval.

How do I check my leave balance?

View entitlement, leave history and approved leave through the Faith HR System.

When is salary paid?

Salary is normally credited on the last working day of the month unless HR communicates a change.

When can I access my payslip?

Your payslip should be available within four working days after salary is credited.

How do I submit a medical claim?

Submit through Faith with the original receipt and Medical Certificate where applicable. Claims approved by the 24th are normally reimbursed in the same cycle.

How do I raise a grievance?

Contact your assigned HR Business Partner or email askhr@daythree.co. Concerns are handled professionally and confidentially.

How do I submit my resignation?

Inform your immediate supervisor, submit the resignation formally and serve the notice period stated in your Employment Contract.

What should I do with a suspicious email?

Do not click links or download attachments. Report it to the IT Helpdesk and follow IT guidance.
