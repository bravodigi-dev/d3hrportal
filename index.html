Payroll

When and how can I request my payslip?

Your payslip will be available within four working days after salary has been credited. If it is not available after this period, email askhr@daythree.co.

How can I set up Zakat deductions?

Register through the official Zakat website for your state and use askhr@daythree.co as the employer email. The Zakat office will notify Daythree and payroll will process the monthly deduction after confirmation.

Can foreign employees claim EPF or SOCSO refunds when leaving Malaysia?

Foreign employees may withdraw EPF contributions when permanently leaving Malaysia, subject to EPF regulations. SOCSO contributions are not refundable.

How does income tax work for foreign employees?

Foreign employees are subject to Malaysian income tax. Residents who stay more than 182 days are generally subject to progressive rates from 0% to 30%. Non-residents are generally subject to a flat 30% rate.

When is salary paid?

Salary is normally credited on the last working day of each month unless otherwise communicated. Employees will be informed in advance of changes caused by public holidays or operational requirements.

How do I update my bank account details?

Submit your request to HR with supporting documents such as a bank statement or account details. The change must be completed before the payroll cut-off date to take effect in the current cycle.

How do I update my EPF nomination?

Update your nomination directly with EPF/KWSP through an EPF branch or its online services where applicable.

How can I request an Employment Confirmation Letter or Salary Letter?

Email askhr@daythree.co and state the letter type, purpose and any specific information required. Allow several working days for processing.

What is EV Portal?

EV Portal means Employee Verification Portal. New candidates use it to verify personal details and support a smooth payroll process.

What is the company’s salary bank panel?

RHB Bank.

Medical Benefits

Can unused medical claims be carried forward?

No. Medical claims operate on a Pay & Claim basis and unused monthly entitlement cannot be carried forward or accumulated.

How do I submit a medical claim?

Submit through the Faith system with the original medical receipt and Medical Certificate where applicable. Claims approved by the 24th are reimbursed in the same payroll cycle; approvals after the cut-off are reimbursed in the following month.

Leave & Working Arrangements

Can I request a special working arrangement as a part-time student?

Employees must follow schedules assigned by Operations. For examinations, apply for Annual Leave, submit the examination timetable, obtain Operations Manager approval and apply through Faith.

How do I apply for Time Off?

Inform your Team Leader, obtain Operations Manager approval and submit through the required process. Maximum Time Off allocation is two hours.

How do I check my leave balance?

Log in to the Faith HR System to view entitlement, leave history and upcoming approved leave. For discrepancies, contact your immediate superior or email HR.

When will my leave be approved?

Approval depends on operational requirements and manager approval. Apply early and do not make travel or personal commitments until the leave is officially approved in Faith.

Can I cancel or amend approved leave?

Yes. Inform your immediate supervisor promptly and update the request in Faith. Changes remain subject to manager approval.

What happens if I fall sick during Annual Leave?

Notify your supervisor and submit a valid Medical Certificate through the prescribed process. The company will review the request according to leave policy.

Performance & Salary

Will there be bonuses or salary increments?

Performance bonuses are not provided because performance is assessed monthly through KPIs. Salary increments are reviewed based on annual performance, business needs and company policy.

Employee Relations

I have a complaint about someone. What should I do?

Speak with your assigned HR Business Partner or email askhr@daythree.co. Concerns are handled professionally, fairly and confidentially.

HR Communication

How can I stay updated with HR announcements?

Regularly check Company Memos in the Faith portal for policy, benefit, event and company updates.

HR Support & Logistics

Who is my HR Business Partner?

Each business unit has an assigned HRBP. Email askhr@daythree.co if you are unsure and HR will direct your enquiry.

How do I update my personal information?

Email HR when your address, phone number, emergency contact, marital status, personal email or educational qualifications change. Supporting documents may be required.

How do I submit my resignation?

Inform your immediate supervisor and submit your resignation. Serve the notice period in your Employment Contract. HR will guide the clearance and exit process.

Who should I contact about a policy?

Contact your HR Business Partner or email askhr@daythree.co.

Career Development

Where can I view internal job vacancies?

Internal vacancies are announced through official company communication channels in the Faith system.

Information Security

What should I do if I receive a suspicious email?

Do not click links or download attachments. Report the email to the IT Helpdesk and delete it only after receiving IT guidance.

Can I use personal email to send company information?

No. Company information must only be shared through approved systems and company email accounts. Unauthorised sharing may result in disciplinary action.
